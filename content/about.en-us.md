## My Role at Ydentic powered by AvePoint

As a Principal Consultant, I support partners in implementing [Ydentic](https://ydentic.com) and unlocking its full potential. My goal is to give partners more time to focus on complex challenges by streamlining and optimizing their core processes. I also help establish HR integrations and work on onboarding partners into the [AvePoint](https://avepoint.com/) [Elements](https://www.avepoint.com/products/elements) platform, ensuring they can use it more intelligently and effectively.

## Focus on User Management and Baselines

My specialization lies in user lifecycle management – from joiners and movers to leavers – with a strong focus on bringing access management as close to the end user as possible. In addition, I contribute to the development of Baselines, an approach that enables Managed Service Providers (MSPs) to quickly identify gaps in their promised service delivery. This gives partners instant insight into areas for improvement.

## Proud of Our Knowledge and Solutions

What I am most proud of is how Ydentic has built deep expertise around the entire user journey within organizations. We combine these insights with technical solutions that reduce the workload for MSPs while simultaneously raising the quality of their services. This empowers our partners to focus on the challenges that truly matter.

## Collaboration in a Strong Team

At Ydentic, we work closely across developers, engineers, project management, and customer success. With short communication lines and extensive knowledge of the underlying platforms, we can respond quickly and provide partners with exactly what they need. This strong blend of expertise and teamwork allows us to deliver real impact at every level.

## Experience and Drive

With over 14 years of experience at the Netherlands’ leading MSP, I bring a wealth of knowledge in standardization, automation, self-service, and security. Today, I use that experience to help Ydentic powered by AvePoint partners grow and professionalize. Ultimately, my drive is to leverage technology in smarter ways to help organizations move forward.
